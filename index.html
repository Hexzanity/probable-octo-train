Nagtabon Beach

Nagtabon Beach is around an hour away from the city, making it the ideal beach destination for those who don't want to travel too far. It has a white sand beach and cerulean waters, and a beautiful beachscape for relaxing and unwinding.

Panagurian Cold Spring

Spend a peaceful day in nature by swimming in the cool, invigorating waters of the Panagurian Cold Spring! As you finish the tour, awe at the spring's stunning blue waters.

Purao Island

Purao Island that is located at Roxas, Palawan. A shallow-dug well provided fresh water in the island. The island features (but is not limited to) a pristine white sand beachfront, very clear blue waters, a stunning sandbar during low tide, and a natural source of fresh water.

Napsan Beach

Napsan Beach is another beach destination that is quiet and peacefully to tour. The closest and most easily accessible west-coast beach from Port Princesa. It is 53 km away from Puerto Princesa.

Balabac Beach

Balabac is a group of 31 islands, many of which are totally uninhabited. You'll find pink-sand beaches, vibrantly colored coral, a wide range of marine life, endemic mouse deer, and welcoming locals. But what you won't find are many other foreigners.
